Flowers

Flowers have always captivated human imagination, symbolizing beauty, love, and the ephemeral nature of life. From the delicate petals of a rose to the vibrant colors of a sunflower, flowers brighten any space with their natural charm.

Each flower tells a unique story, often tied to cultural meanings or historical significance. For instance, the lotus flower is revered in many Eastern traditions for its symbolism of purity and enlightenment, while the daisy, with its white petals and yellow center, represents innocence and new beginnings.
